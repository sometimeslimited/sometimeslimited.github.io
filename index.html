Bento Blocks

Bento Blocks is a puzzle game that combines culinary creativity with brain-teasing challenges. Slice, arrange, and perfect your bento-making skills as you craft delicious lunch boxes at your own pace.

Complete The Recipe Book

Solve each puzzle with the fewest cuts to complete your recipe book.

Delightful Discoveries

Discover a fresh take on puzzle games as you slice and arrange ingredients to craft bento boxes.

Hand-Crafted Levels

Explore over 100 lovingly hand-crafted levels, each with its own unique twist and satisfying solution.
