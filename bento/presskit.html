Factsheet

Developer: Sometimes Limited

Based in: New Zealand

Platform: PC (Steam)

Game Summary:
Bento Blocks is a puzzle game that combines culinary creativity with brain-teasing challenges. Slice, arrange, and perfect your bento-making skills as you craft delicious lunch boxes at your own pace.

Description

  • Discover a fresh take on puzzle games as you slice and arrange ingredients to craft bento boxes.
  • Explore new puzzle mechanics and techniques to complete your recipe book.
  • Savor the challenge of each new puzzle and mechanic as you progress through various recipes.
  • Explore over 100+ hand-crafted levels, each with its own unique twist and satisfying solution.
  • Unwind with a game that blends culinary arts with cozy and relaxing puzzles.
  • Relaxing music and calming visuals.
